About

About Me

I am Sinan Ergen, a Research Assistant in the Department of Mathematics at Balıkesir University. I am currently pursuing my M.Sc. in Mathematics at Hacettepe University.

My academic work is mainly centered on finite element methods, computational fluid dynamics, fluid mechanics, scientific computing, and numerical analysis.

In particular, I am interested in numerical solution methods, iterative algorithms, and performance improvement techniques for the Navier–Stokes equations and natural convection problems. This website provides an overview of my academic background, research interests, publications, seminars, awards, simulation outputs, and professional links.

Research

Research Interests

Finite Element Methods

Numerical solution of partial differential equations using finite element techniques and their theoretical and computational aspects.

Computational Fluid Dynamics

Numerical modeling, simulation, and performance analysis of fluid flow problems.

Fluid Mechanics

Mathematical models describing fluid behavior and their applied analysis.

Scientific Computing

Efficient and accurate computational approaches for solving mathematical and engineering problems.

Numerical Analysis

Convergence, stability, error analysis, and the theoretical foundations of numerical methods.

Navier–Stokes and Natural Convection

Numerical methods, iterative schemes, and computational strategies for flow and heat transfer problems.
