About

I am Sinan Ergen, a Research Assistant in the Department of Mathematics at Balıkesir University. I am currently pursuing my M.Sc. in Mathematics at Hacettepe University.

My academic interests are mainly focused on finite element methods, computational fluid dynamics, fluid mechanics, scientific computing, numerical analysis, and numerical methods for the Navier–Stokes and natural convection equations.

This homepage provides a concise overview of my academic background, research interests, selected publication, and professional links.
