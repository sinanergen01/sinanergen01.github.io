About

About Me

I am Sinan Ergen, a Research Assistant in the Department of Mathematics at Balıkesir University. I am also pursuing my M.Sc. in Mathematics at Hacettepe University. My academic work is mainly centered on finite element methods, computational fluid dynamics, scientific computing, and numerical analysis.

In particular, I am interested in numerical solution methods, iterative algorithms, and performance improvement techniques for the Navier–Stokes equations and natural convection problems. This website provides a concise overview of my research interests, academic background, professional profiles, and selected publications.

Research Interests

Finite Element Methods

Scientific Computing

Efficient and accurate computational approaches for solving mathematical and engineering problems.

Numerical Analysis

Convergence, stability, error analysis, and the theoretical foundations of numerical methods.

Navier–Stokes and Natural Convection

Numerical methods, iterative schemes, and computational strategies for flow and heat transfer problems.
